    Who we are

    Coros is a long-term oriented real estate company that aims to identify unused potential in urban areas and to create sustainable value for modern communities with future-oriented utilization concepts.

    1

    Our approach is characterized by transparency and dialog in direct exchange with key stakeholders from business, politics and the community itself in order to ensure the best possible integration of our projects into their respective urban context.

    Learn more about our approach.

    coros approach
    2

    We are an integrated real estate company that covers all relevant services along the real estate value chain with high quality. This enables us to optimally develop the individual potential of a property and actively shape our added value.
